Add small-screen support to the date+time range controls. On phones (≤599px) a linked `forge-date-time-field` renders a tappable two-line value display (start over end) with the duration, and `forge-date-time-picker` opens as a full-height `forge-bottom-sheet` — a horizontal preset chip rail, a single calendar, stacked time inputs, and a pinned Apply/Cancel footer — instead of the popover. Desktop behavior is unchanged.
