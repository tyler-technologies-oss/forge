# Skeleton

The skeleton component is a generic, low-level component that can be used to build loading placeholder pages/elements called “skeleton screens”. These screens represent the block-level visual elements of a page, or section of a page, where content is intended to be loaded sometime in the future. Using skeleton screens gives the illusion of speed to the user, and can often reduce frustration for users during long running processes.

There are a few built-in variants that can be used by adding an attribute to the element. These built-in variants, are specifically designed to mimic other components in the Forge library for ease of use.

This component will produce a grey colored element, with a subtle animation over the to show that the content is loading.

## Properties/Attributes

<PropertyDef name="avatar" type="string" defaultValue="''" prop={false}>

  Mimics the avatar component.

</PropertyDef>

<PropertyDef name="list-item" type="string" defaultValue="''" prop={false}>

  Matches the styles of an individual list-item.

</PropertyDef>

<PropertyDef name="text" type="string" defaultValue="''" prop={false}>

  Intended to give the impression that there is textual content in this location.

</PropertyDef>

<PropertyDef name="stretch" type="string" defaultValue="''" prop={false}>

  Intended to mimic larger sections of content where the skeleton will stretch to fit its parent bounds.

</PropertyDef>

<PropertyDef name="chip" type="string" defaultValue="''" prop={false}>

  Represents an individual chip-styled placeholder.

</PropertyDef>

<PropertyDef name="button" type="string" defaultValue="''" prop={false}>

  Represents an individual button-styled placeholder.

</PropertyDef>

<PropertyDef name="form-field" type="string" defaultValue="''" prop={false}>

  Represents a form field such as text-field, select, or autocomplete... etc.

</PropertyDef>

## CSS custom properties

| Name                                   | Description
| :--------------------------------------| :----------------
| `--forge-skeleton-height`                | Controls the internal height of the skeleton container.
| `--forge-skeleton-margin`                | Controls the margin around the internal skeleton container element.
| `--forge-skeleton-border-radius`         | Controls the border radius of the element. Defaults to `4px`.
| `--forge-skeleton-theme-background`      | Controls the background-color of the internal skeleton element.
| `--forge-skeleton-avatar-size`           | Controls the height and width of the internal skeleton container to mimic avatar.
| `--forge-skeleton-button-height`         | Controls the height of the internal skeleton container to mimic button.
| `--forge-skeleton-button-width`          | Controls the width of the internal skeleton container to mimic button.
| `--forge-skeleton-form-field-height`     | Controls the height of the internal skeleton container to mimic form field.
| `--forge-skeleton-form-field-width`      | Controls the width of the internal skeleton container to mimic form field.
| `--forge-skeleton-chip-height`           | Controls the height of the internal skeleton container to mimic chip.
| `--forge-skeleton-chip-width`            | Controls the width of the internal skeleton container to mimic chip.
| `--forge-skeleton-list-item-height`      | Controls the height of the internal skeleton container to mimic list item.
| `--forge-skeleton-list-item-margin`      | Controls the margin of the internal skeleton container to mimic list item.
| `--forge-skeleton-text-height`           | Controls the height of the internal skeleton container to mimic text.
| `--forge-skeleton-theme-gradient`        | Controls the background gradient of the internal skeleton container during the animation.
